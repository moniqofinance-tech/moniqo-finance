Members

Moniqo is an organization with a mission to empower the next generation with the tools to master financial literacy, one lesson at a time. The organization exists to build economic confidence in youth and cultivate a future where smart financial decision-making starts early.

Our Vision

At Moniqo, we envision a world where every young person has the knowledge and skills to make informed financial decisions, setting them up for a lifetime of financial well-being. We are committed to providing accessible and engaging financial education to empower the leaders of tomorrow.

Who We Serve

Moniqo is dedicated to serving youth from diverse backgrounds, including underserved communities, schools, and youth organizations. We are passionate about reaching those who may not have easy access to financial literacy resources, ensuring that every young person has the opportunity to build a strong financial foundation.

Our Approach

We approach financial education through interactive and practical learning experiences. By collaborating with schools, community centers, and other partners, we deliver workshops, mentorship programs, and online resources to make learning about money management engaging and impactful for young people.
